# Common Login Problems

- Verify username and password; check for keyboard layout changes.
- If SSO is enabled, confirm the IdP is reachable and your session is valid.
- For lockouts after many attempts, wait 30 minutes or contact support to unlock.
- Clear browser cache or try an incognito/private window.
- Check service status page for incidents.
