# Password Reset

If you cannot reset your password, check the following:

1. Verify the reset email did not land in the spam folder.
2. The reset link expires in 15 minutes; request a new link if needed.
3. Corporate email filters may block messages; ask IT to whitelist the sender.

# Login Issues

When users cannot log in:

- Ensure the account is active and not locked due to too many attempts.
- Check if SSO is enabled and the identity provider is reachable.
- Clear browser cache or try an incognito window.

# Ticket Priority Guidance

- urgent: Outage or severe impact, fix immediately.
- high: Business-critical feature affected, fix within 4 hours.
- medium: Standard issue, address within 1 business day.
- low: Minor request or UI issue, schedule accordingly.
